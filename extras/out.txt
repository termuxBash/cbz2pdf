Tama is a Japanese word that can mean several things depending on the context.  The most common meanings include:

* **Soul/Spirit:** This is a common, albeit somewhat poetic, usage.
* **Ball:** As in a ball-shaped object.
* **Pearl:**  Referring to a gem.
* **Egg:** In some dialects.
